Section 01

Who We Are

NORTHCORP is a privately held commodity trading and mining contractor operating out of the Kurukana system. We provide haulage, carrier logistics, and resource extraction services to established organizations across the Core Systems and beyond.

Founded by CEO Kalvin North, NORTHCORP is built on old-fashioned principles: work hard, deliver on time, and never make a promise you cannot keep. We may be a small operation, but we handle every contract with the same discipline and attention to detail expected from a fleet ten times our size.

Currently operating a Type-9 Heavy for bulk haulage between systems and a Panther Clipper Mk II for short-range in-system hauling.

NORTHCORP is not currently accepting employee applications. All crew positions are filled.
